Welcome to kebab-case vs CamelCase

In this experiment, we aim to collect data regarding the readability of kebab-case vs CamelCase. You will be prompted a brief sentence written in plain english and a variety of similar options written in either kebab-case or CamelCase. Your objective is to select the correct conversion of the plain sentence.
To get you accustomed to the experiment format, below you can find an example set of options. The original plain sentence is "paint wall". Click the buttons to verify which one is the correct answer.
Once you clicked both correct answers for the case and completed the quick form, the experiment will begin.
